About

What is Oddity Hub?

Oddity Hub is a small student‑focused writing service. You share your content (text, photos, or PDF), and we copy it into a notebook or file so you can submit it in school.

We understand that not everyone is comfortable with handwriting. Some students write slowly or feel stressed before submission dates.

• We only write what you send, in a clear and simple style.
• You decide the page size, margin style, and headings.
• Useful for school records, fair copies, and charts.

Conditions

Please read before you book

These simple rules keep everything clear for both sides. By using Oddity Hub, you agree to the conditions below.

  • 1
    We only write for you.
    We do not create or correct your content. You must provide the text or material that needs to be written.
  • 2
    No responsibility for teacher checking.
    Our duty is handwriting only. If your teacher does not check, grade, or accept the work, Oddity Hub is not responsible.
  • 3
    Delivery time: 1–7 days.
    Delivery usually takes between 1 and 7 days depending on your location, number of pages, and our current bookings.
  • 4
    First party advance: 40%.
    You pay 40% of the total amount in advance to confirm your booking. The remaining amount is paid at the time of delivery.
Tip: Book your pages a few days before your submission date so we can write calmly without rushing.

Friendly, but clear.

We want every student to have access to handwritten pages when they need them. These conditions help us keep the work honest, smooth, and respectful for everyone involved.
